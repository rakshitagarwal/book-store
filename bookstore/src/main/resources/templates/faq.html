Shopping

Q: How do I place an order?

A: First you need to set up an account, then you need to fill the billing and shipping details. Once the account is set up, you are ready to place an order.

Q: How do I set up an account? 

A: Click on the "MY ACCOUNT" button in the upper right hand corner of the main page and then click on "Create new account" tab. If you are an existing user, then go to the "Log in" tab.

Q: How long does it take to process and ship orders?

A:  It usually takes about 1 - 2 business days (Monday-Friday) for order processing regardless of the shipping method chosen. Once shipped you should receive your order within 2 - 4 business days in case of Ground shipping, and 1-2 business days in case of Premium shipping.

Q: Are all of the items guaranteed in stock?

A:  We try to update the website as often as possible to reflect out of stock conditions. However, the ability to order a book does not guarantee that it will be in stock when your order is processed. You will be notified if your order is not available.

Q: I can't find a specific item on the website, are there items available that are not listed on the website?

A: The books that are currently shown on our website represent what is currently available to view and order online. If a book has gone out of stock then it will not be shown in the Bookshelf Menu to the user

Q: How can I check the status of my order?

A: Click on "MY ACCOUNT" and login with your registered email address and password. You can view the status of any orders that have previously made using the same account. You can also contact us at 956298547 with your name, order number, and a request for status of your order. Our office hours are Monday - Friday 8am -4:30pm IST.

Shipping 

Q: How much is the shipping charge on orders?

A: There are two categories of shipping namely, Ground shipping, which cost Rs.20, and Premium shipping, which costs Rs.40.

Q. What is your shipping method?

A: We ship using Indian Post Service, DTDC, Blue Dart and DHL.

Q: When will I receive my order?

A:It usually takes about 1 - 2 business days (Monday-Friday) for order processing regardless of the shipping method chosen. Once shipped you should receive your order within 2 - 4 business days in case of Ground shipping, and 1-2 business days in case of Premium shipping.
